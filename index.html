All 4 connectivity sub-types degraded. From customer's POV, these are the same problem: "my internet isn't working properly." TAT rules split them across Service and Others, but the partner resolution work is the same. Investigate together.

What to investigate

  • ISD: Volume stable, rate drifted. Field response time slowing? Technician availability?
  • RDNI: Sharpest rate drop. Check recharge → activation → router sync flow.
  • Frequent Disconnection & Slow Speed: Degraded-experience tickets. Cross-ref router uptime / ping flap.
  • Partner cohort: Small partners (20–99 Dec tickets) had the biggest rate drop (−8.8pp). Capacity / training issue?
